Apple Turnovers

  • Prep Filling: Melt butter in a saucepan, add apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Cook until soft, stir in flour, and let cool.
  • Preheat Oven: Preheat to 375째F (190째C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Assemble Turnovers: Roll out puff pastry, cut into squares, spoon apple filling onto one half of each square. Fold pastry over filling, press edges to seal.
  • Egg Wash and Bake: Brush turnovers with beaten egg, sprinkle with sugar. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  • Serve: Let cool on baking sheet, transfer to wire rack to cool completely. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Italian Stuffed Peppers

  • Prep Peppers: Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C). Place pepper halves in a baking dish, cut side up.
  • Cook Sausage: In a skillet, cook Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Drain excess fat.
  • Mix Filling: In a bowl, combine cooked sausage, cooked rice, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  • Stuff Peppers: Spoon filling mixture into each pepper half, pressing down gently.
  • Bake: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until peppers are tender.
  • Serve: Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving. Enjoy your Italian stuffed peppers hot!
